Learn to read price action through candlestick patterns. Each pattern is drawn exactly as it appears on a real chart — with context, reliability scores, and a quiz.

WHAT IS A CANDLESTICK?
Each candle shows 4 prices in a period:
🔼 High — top of wick
🔽 Low — bottom of wick
🟢 Open/Close — the body
Green = price went up · Red = went down
⚠️ Note: Patterns are tools, not guarantees. Always confirm with volume and context.
